CLASS 01 — RECREATIONAL BOATS

Boats

Runabouts, bass boats, pontoons, center consoles, and sailboats. This is the report most private buyers order before a deposit changes hands.

HIN decode
Confirms manufacturer, model year, and hull series from the hull identification number.
Title & registration status
Current title state and whether registration is active, lapsed, or flagged.
Lien & loan records
Outstanding finance claims that could follow the boat after sale.
Theft record check
Cross-referenced against the stolen-vessel registers available for the market.
Damage & total-loss history
Recorded insurance write-offs, salvage titles, or flood damage.
Ownership history
Number of registered owners and the state or province of each registration.
Typical use: a private-party purchase where the buyer wants title and theft status confirmed before wiring a deposit.

CLASS 02 — MOTOR & SAILING YACHTS

Yachts

Motor yachts and sailing yachts, typically higher-value purchases where survey history and ownership chain carry more weight.

Documentation & registry status
Current flag state or documentation number and whether it's in good standing.
Mortgage & lien filings
Marine mortgages and maritime liens recorded against the vessel.
Insurance-loss history
Recorded claims, groundings, fire, or storm-damage events where disclosed.
Ownership & charter history
Chain of registered ownership, including any commercial charter registration.
Prior survey references
Notes any surveys on file with the registry or classification society.
Theft & law-enforcement flags
Checked against the stolen-vessel and law-enforcement registers we can reach.
Typical use: pre-purchase due diligence ahead of a marine survey, or documentation for a lender.

CLASS 03 — PERSONAL WATERCRAFT

Jet Skis & PWCs

Jet skis and other personal watercraft, often bought and sold quickly and privately — where a fast, focused check matters most.

HIN decode
Confirms manufacturer, model, and model year from the hull identification number.
Registration status
Whether the registration is active, expired, or reported as non-renewable.
Theft record check
PWCs are a frequently targeted class — checked against available stolen registers.
Lien records
Outstanding finance agreements tied to the registered owner.
Damage & total-loss flags
Insurance write-off or salvage status where recorded.
Ownership count
Number of registered owners since the watercraft was first documented.
Typical use: a quick pre-purchase check on a same-day private sale or marketplace listing.

CLASS 04 — COMMERCIAL & WORKING VESSELS

Commercial Vessels

Fishing boats, workboats, charter and passenger craft — where licensing, casualty history, and inspection records matter as much as title.

Commercial registration & licensing
Confirms the vessel's registered commercial-use status and endorsements.
Lien & mortgage filings
Maritime liens and financing claims recorded against the vessel.
Casualty & incident reports
Recorded groundings, collisions, or reportable marine casualties.
Inspection & survey status
Where public, the vessel's current standing with its inspecting authority.
Ownership & operator history
Chain of registered ownership and any change in operating company.
Theft & law-enforcement flags
Checked against stolen-vessel and enforcement registers where available.
Typical use: due diligence for a fleet purchase, charter operator acquisition, or commercial lending decision.
