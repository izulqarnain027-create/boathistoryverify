Where we operate

Built for a global buyer, not one country's registry.

Wherever the vessel is registered, we run the same standard: pull every source we can legally reach, mark anything unverifiable as unverifiable, and never guess at a clean history.

Reach

Every country we can serve

We accept orders for vessels registered anywhere in the world and check every registry, database, and public record we can legally access for that market.

Turnaround

Every report — regardless of the vessel's home country — is emailed to you within 4–8 hours of confirmed payment.

How it works

Three steps, one document, no guesswork.

SUBMIT

Give us the identifier

HIN, hull number, registration, or documentation number — whatever the listing shows. We'll tell you if we need anything else.

VERIFY

Title and registration status, lien and loan filings, theft and law-enforcement records, and documented damage or total-loss history.

DELIVER

You get a plain-language PDF

Confirmed items are marked clear. Anything we couldn't verify is flagged as such — never filled in with a guess.
